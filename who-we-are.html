Who We Are Introduction

Komconsult Sdn. Bhd. is a successful Malaysian ICT system integrator and solutions provider which also 100% BUMIPUTERA Company. We are focuses in areas of Healthcare IT, IT Security, Hospitality, and Utility. We are proven track records since 1991 and have a strong core values. Komconsult have strong partnership with clients & technology providers.

The solutions include communication network, security surveillance, system design, specialized IT application development, etc. We have vast experience in the field of communication, electronic equipment, system design, IT application development, etc.
